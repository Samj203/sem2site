About Us

Welcome to Harmony Cafe & Restaurant, where every meal is a step towards a more sustainable future.

Founded by newlyweds Mindy and Mike, we are on a mission to combat food waste and ensure everyone has access to nourishing meals. Drawing on their experience in a successful sustainable farming family business, Mindy and Mike have established a nonprofit organization dedicated to tackling the issue of food waste generated by local supermarkets and distributors.

Located in the heart of our city, our restaurant and cafe operate on a 'Pay As You Feel' basis. By partnering with local supermarkets and distributors, we create a daily menu using diverted fresh food and other ingredients that would otherwise end up in landfills. Our goal is simple: to divert food waste into a sustainable restaurant environment where everyone, regardless of their financial situation, can enjoy a nourishing meal.

In addition to our restaurant, we also offer a 'Pay As You Feel' food box filled with fresh, diverted food from local grocery stores and farmers markets. Available for pick-up at select locations within the city on certain days, our food box reservations are essential due to limited availability. We promote the food box within community hubs to ensure it reaches those who need it most, aiming to provide nourishing food to struggling individuals and families while upholding dignity and autonomy.

At Harmony Cafe & Restaurant, we believe in the power of food to bring people together and create positive change. Join us in our mission to build a more sustainable and compassionate community, one meal at a time.
